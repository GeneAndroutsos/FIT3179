Australian Climate Visualisations (1975 – 2019)

Exploring Australia's temperature trends across all 112 Australian Climate Observations Reference Network - Surface Air Temperature (ACORN-SAT) weather stations.

The dataset provides a holistic climate outlook for Australia from 1975 to 2019, including maximum yearly average temperature, elevation, and state distribution of all ACORN weather stations.

Data Source: Bureau of Meteorology (BOM), Kaggle | Created for FIT3179 Data Visualisation 2 | Author: Gene Androutsos, 2025
